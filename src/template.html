About me

Born in Punjab, India, and raised in the concrete, urban jungle of New York City, I've always been on the hunt for bigger and better game. I found myself constantly bored with classes, and it felt like I was getting put in a box - from one set of expectations to another - none of them fit me or who I wanted to be. I started looking around for other options, something that would give me freedom to explore ideas I had already been thinking about my entire life. Instead of just day-dreaming at a desk, I decided to dive into an intensive coding bootcamp. After spending nine weeks abroad at Le Wagon Bali, an intensive course which covered all aspects of fullstack web development, I have greatly broadened my capabilities and am ready to do even more.
